The University of Washington has formally launched HoverBoard, the UW's electronic IACUC protocol management system.

OAW Mission and Vision

Protect the integrity and excellence of vertebrate animal research and teaching at the University of Washington.Provide comprehensive resources and outstanding services to the UW IACUC, faculty, staff, and other partners for navigating and complying with the rules and regulations regarding use of vertebrate animals.

Our Service

The Office of Animal Welfare (OAW) facilitates the IACUC review of research protocols and grants involving live vertebrate animals. In addition, the OAW provides oversight and compliance on behalf of the IACUC in accordance with applicable laws, policies and regulations. Our office hours are Monday through Friday, 8:30 AM – 5:00 PM.

HoverBoard

HoverBoard provides a web-based, end-to-end solution for investigators to create, submit and manage animal protocols and for the Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC) to review and approve them. HoverBoard is an important initiative of the Office of Animal Welfare in partnership with the Office of Research Information Systems, to streamline workflow and simplify management of compliant IACUC ​Processes.​

Process for Reporting Concerns About Care and Use of Animals

The University of Washington investigates any allegations of suspected misuse of animals or deficiences regarding the care and use of research or teaching animals. An individual reporting a suspected deficiency need not idenfity him/herself to make a report. To make a report or discuss a concern or perceived problem, please call 206.685.7363
