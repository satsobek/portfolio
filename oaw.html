Process for Reporting Concerns About Care and Use of Animals

The University of Washington investigates any allegations of suspected misuse of animals or deficiences regarding the care and use of research or teaching animals. An individual reporting a suspected deficiency need not idenfity him/herself to make a report. To make a report or discuss a concern or perceived problem, please call 206.685.7363
